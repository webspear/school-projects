Rock, Paper, Scissors!

HOW TO PLAY

1. Basic Rules:
Rock beats Scissors.
Scissors beats Paper.
Paper beats Rock.

2. Health
You start off with 100 health points. After each loss, you will receive a health penalty depending on the difficulty, as listed below:
Easy: 10 HP lost.
Normal: 20 HP lost.
Hard: 50 HP lost.

3. Points
Each win gives you 10 points. Your goal is to get the most amount of points before you lose all your health.
