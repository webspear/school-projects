HOW TO PLAY

How to play

This is a wave based game where you defend the castle from incoming ennemies.


Enemies

In order to defeat an enemy, you will have to type the matching word that is associated to it.


Stats

When the enemy comes into contact with the castle wall, your health (castle integrity) reduces.
Each time an enemy is defeated, points are obtained.
Points can be exchanged for upgrades or repairs at the shop between waves.


Waves

There are five waves in total, with each increasing in difficulty.
At later waves, you will encounter bosses, which have longer words.
